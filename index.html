What is Game Changer? 

Game Changer is a short online video that  covers the experiences of women within gaming spaces and highlights the verbal attacks that theaten to sabotage one of their favourite hobbies. This video outlines the ongoing issues that people who go through online and the abuse women suffer simply because of their gender identity.

This is all backed up through interviews with gamers from varying levels of experience. Also, with the backing of opinions from professionals and shocking extracts from actual in game experiences we wish to shine a light on those who are often outspoken and aggressively pushed out of the competitive gaming sphere.
